# meet.me — downloads

**Privacy-first meeting transcription for macOS.** Records your meetings, transcribes and diarizes them entirely on your Mac, and writes clean summaries — without your audio ever leaving the device.

## Download

**[⬇️ Download the latest release](https://github.com/yunomiwell/meetme-releases/releases/latest)** — a signed, notarized `.dmg`. Opens cleanly on macOS 13+.

The app checks here for updates automatically and offers to install new versions.

## What it does

- 🎙️ Records mic + system audio locally — no bot joins your call
- 📝 On-device transcription (strong Hungarian + English code-switch)
- 👥 Speaker diarization + enrollment
- 🧠 Summaries on-device (Apple Foundation Models) or via your own Claude key
- 🔒 Audio + voiceprints never leave your Mac; only summary text crosses the network, only on request
